Here's a simple recipe for hot tea:\n\nIngredients:\n- 1 tea bag (black, green, herbal, or your preferred flavor)\n- 1 cup of boiling water\n- Optional: sweetener (sugar, honey, etc.)\n- Optional: milk or lemon wedge (depending on your taste preference)\n\nInstructions:\n1. Start by boiling water in a kettle or saucepan until it reaches a rolling boil.\n\n2. While the water is boiling, place a tea bag into a cup or mug.\n\n3. Pour the boiling water over the tea bag in the cup.\n\n4. Let the tea steep for about 3-5 minutes, or adjust the steeping time based on the type of tea and your preference for a stronger or milder flavor.\n\n5. After the desired steeping time, remove the tea bag from the cup and discard it.\n\n6. If desired, add sweetener to taste. Stir until dissolved.\n\n7. Optional: You can also add a splash of milk for a creamy tea or squeeze in a lemon wedge for a citrusy flavor. Adjust the amount based on your preference.\n\n8. Give your tea a gentle stir to combine any added ingredients.\n\n9. Allow the tea to cool slightly before enjoying your hot cup of tea.\n\nFeel free to experiment with different types of tea, flavors, and additions to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy your comforting cup of hot tea!
